# 02 · Product Architecture — Digital Code / Zerkalo

## Product layers

### 1. First Mirror

Entry through date.

Purpose:

- create trust;
- show the formula;
- produce the first recognition;
- move to lead.

### 2. Personal Myth

Entry through state and image.

Purpose:

- reflect current inner state;
- create emotional contact;
- give one soft step;
- move to lead.

### 3. Big Research

Main paid product.

Purpose:

- deliver full personal value;
- create premium client experience;
- produce testimonials and reference files;
- become the basis for future automation.

### 4. Telegram bot

Return and delivery channel.

Purpose:

- open Zerkalo;
- notify owner;
- continue dialogue;
- deliver future reports;
- support follow-up.

## Important distinction

Zerkalo and Telegram bot are not competitors.

```text
Zerkalo creates the first premium experience.
Telegram bot keeps the relationship alive.
```
