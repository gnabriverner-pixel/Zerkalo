# 06_EXAMPLE_OUTPUT — Big Research Prompt Pack

This is not a full report. It is a compact style reference for the Big Research voice.

## Input example

```text
Name: Artem
Date: 06.05.1986
Formula: 6 — 8 — 11 — 5 — 1
Key compounds: 35/8, 41/5, 82/10/1
```

## Short numeric passport fragment

Your formula begins with 6 and moves toward 1 through 8, 11, and 5.

This is not a soft formula, even if the first number is connected with beauty, relationship, and quality. The inner start asks for harmony, taste, contact, and emotional precision. But the path immediately brings the person into the territory of structure, pressure, management, and result.

A short working phrase:

```text
quality must learn structure, structure must learn speech, and speech must return the person to a clear center.
```

## Main pattern fragment

The main tension is not a lack of talent. The more exact question is whether the talent has a form strong enough to hold it.

There is an inner need for beauty, meaning, contact, and living atmosphere. But the route does not allow staying only in sensitivity. It asks for material structure: decisions, boundaries, repeatable process, and visible result.

When this formula is not assembled, the person may live between two poles: either dissolving in feeling and image, or trying to force reality through pressure. Neither pole gives the mature result. The mature route is different: give feeling a structure, give structure a voice, and give the voice one clear direction.

## Practical route fragment

For the next 30 days, the useful experiment is not to start ten new directions. It is to choose one format where your experience can be packaged and offered.

Three support points:

1. Reduce the product to one clear promise.
2. Make the first version manually, not automatically.
3. Use Telegram and Zerkalo as a connected entry, not as competing products.

One main step:

```text
Create one paid offer and show it to five people before building another feature.
```

## Living Word fragment

You do not need another sign that the project is meaningful. You have enough signs. The question now is simpler and harder: what form will finally carry it?

Your formula does not ask you to become colder. It asks you to stop confusing depth with unfinishedness. Depth becomes power only when it receives a vessel.

The vessel now is not a perfect platform. It is one clear product, one living offer, one finished document, one person who pays because they recognize themselves in the mirror.
