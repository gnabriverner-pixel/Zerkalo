# 03_STYLE_RULES — Big Research Prompt Pack

## 1. Main style

Premium editorial, not mystical performance.

The text should feel:

- calm;
- exact;
- alive;
- personal;
- structured;
- visually readable;
- useful after reading.

## 2. Sentence rhythm

Use a mix of:

- short recognition sentences;
- medium explanatory sentences;
- occasional strong final lines.

Avoid long abstract paragraphs without a practical anchor.

## 3. Good paragraph pattern

```text
Recognition.
Why the formula shows it.
How it appears in life.
What the person can do with it.
```

## 4. Avoid generic praise

Do not write:

- “You are a unique person.”
- “You have great potential.”
- “You are very deep.”
- “You are strong and talented.”

Instead, make the recognition specific:

```text
Your formula does not show a lack of strength. It shows a repeated problem of strength without a stable form.
```

## 5. Avoid filler expressions

Reduce:

- “it is important to note”;
- “this suggests that”;
- “in this context”;
- “as mentioned earlier”;
- “one might say”.

## 6. Russian style guidance

For Russian output:

- avoid bureaucratic syntax;
- avoid overusing “является”;
- avoid repeated “глубокий” / “сильный” / “внутренний”;
- avoid awkward imported AI phrasing;
- prefer direct human rhythm.

## 7. Premium does not mean decorative

Premium means:

- fewer words;
- sharper thought;
- better structure;
- no panic;
- no cheap excitement;
- no pseudo-ancient fog.

## 8. Closing line rule

Every major section should end with a useful conclusion, not only a beautiful sentence.

Good:

```text
For this formula, the first step is not to add more force, but to give existing force a clear rhythm.
```

Weak:

```text
Therefore, you should believe in yourself and move toward your destiny.
```
