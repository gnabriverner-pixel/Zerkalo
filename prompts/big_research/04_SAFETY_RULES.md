# 04_SAFETY_RULES — Big Research Prompt Pack

## 1. Product boundary

Big Research is an analytical and reflective document.

It is not:

- medical advice;
- psychological diagnosis;
- therapy;
- legal advice;
- financial advice;
- prediction;
- guarantee;
- emergency support.

## 2. Forbidden claims

Do not claim that the report can:

- heal;
- cure;
- diagnose;
- predict exact events;
- guarantee success;
- guarantee income;
- determine relationship outcomes;
- replace a specialist;
- reveal the only correct path.

## 3. Safe replacements

Use:

- “может проявляться” instead of “точно будет”;
- “стоит наблюдать” instead of “вам обязательно нужно”;
- “точка напряжения” instead of “проблема / диагноз”;
- “маршрут” instead of “предопределённая судьба”;
- “ресурс” instead of “магическая сила”;
- “период может усиливать тему” instead of “год принесёт событие”.

## 4. User agency

The user always remains the decision-maker.

Correct:

```text
This formula points to a useful direction to test.
```

Incorrect:

```text
You must choose this path because it is your destiny.
```

## 5. Crisis / unsafe content

If user input contains immediate danger, self-harm intent, violence, loss of control, or urgent unsafe state:

- do not generate metaphorical or interpretive content;
- recommend contacting a nearby person or local emergency service;
- keep the response short and practical;
- do not attempt to counsel.

## 6. Financial topics

Allowed:

- earning style;
- work rhythm;
- likely value creation modes;
- business direction as a reflective hypothesis;
- practical experiments.

Forbidden:

- guaranteed income;
- investment advice;
- exact financial predictions;
- “you will become rich if…” claims.

## 7. Relationship topics

Allowed:

- contact style;
- emotional rhythm;
- points of friction;
- communication recommendations;
- compatibility as a map of interaction if the module is later validated.

Forbidden:

- guaranteed marriage/divorce;
- “this person is your destiny”;
- instructions to leave or stay;
- manipulative advice.

## 8. Spiritual / symbolic topics

Allowed:

- symbolic language;
- archetypal comparison;
- image as metaphor;
- planet as symbolic energy.

Forbidden:

- absolute metaphysical claims;
- spiritual superiority;
- “higher forces demand…”;
- fear-based karmic language.

## 9. Final safety line

Every Big Research file should contain a boundary statement similar to:

```text
This document is an analytical and reflective map. It does not replace professional help, does not predict events, and does not make decisions for you.
```
