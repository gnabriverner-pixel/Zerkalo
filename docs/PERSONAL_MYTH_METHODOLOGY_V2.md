## 1. NARRATIVE ARC (EIT Linde + Fairy-tale Therapy + Campbell/Jung)

### 10-Step Story Structure:
1. **Обыденный мир** — The hero's ordinary life. Sensory grounding. 3-4 truisms (Yes Set).
2. **Соматическая тревога** — A sensation appears in the body/landscape (mirrors Q1 input).
3. **Образ проявляется** — The sensation becomes a visible creature/object/phenomenon (mirrors Q2).
4. **Зов к путешествию** — The disruption forces the hero to move.
5. **Встреча с Помощником** — A wise figure (Inner Wise One / Caring Parent archetype) appears.
6. **Испытание и Диалог с Образом** — The hero meets the tension-image face to face. Sits beside it, gives it space, thanks its protective role.
7. **Трансформация** — Through recognition and acceptance, the image transforms. The hidden resource (Q4) is revealed.
8. **Дар** — The hero receives an inner gift — an internal quality that was always there.
9. **Возвращение** — The hero returns to the ordinary world, changed. The landscape mirrors the inner healing.
10. **Закрепление** — A practical one-step anchor connecting the fairy tale to the reader's real life.
