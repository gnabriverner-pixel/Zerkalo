# The Mirror Opens — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Contemporary editorial system on warm tactile paper: quiet, intelligent, human-scale and precise. The visual language should feel like a premium book spread that gradually reveals a structure hidden inside ordinary data. Numbers are evidence, not mysticism. Motion is deliberate and calm, with clear hierarchy and no spectacle for its own sake.

## Colors

- `#F4F0E7` — warm paper background
- `#1D1A16` — primary ink
- `#6E675E` — secondary graphite
- `#B69352` — restrained antique gold accent
- `#D8D0C1` — soft structural line

## Typography

- Display: `Cormorant Garamond`, serif fallback
- Interface / labels: `Inter`, sans-serif fallback

## Motion

- Calm weighted entrances, 0.45–0.9s.
- Structure assembles progressively; no bouncing or playful elastic motion.
- Use movement to explain hierarchy: raw date → five functions → connected route → human meaning → formula evidence.
- Final frame holds long enough to read.

## What NOT to Do

- No zodiac, tarot, constellations, auras or cosmic particles.
- No purple-blue AI gradients.
- No fake expert avatar or talking head.
- No decorative number explosions.
- No mystical claims or deterministic personality labels.
